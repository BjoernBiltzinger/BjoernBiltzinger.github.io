preview_text: Presenting a time-resolved spectral catalog of INTEGRAL/SPI detected gamma-ray bursts (GRBs). We show that INTEGRAL/SPI can constrain GRB spectra best, when the peak energy is in the energy range 100-400 keV. Also shows that synchrotron emission can fit the majority of the GRBs well and we see emission periods in the slow cooling regime, which challenges current theoretical GRB models.
